About Us

Travel Goals is an online site aimed to guide its users when planning for vacation tours and discovering destinations. It was founded in Rivers State, Nigeria. We decided to create this as our first project because we had an urge to bring all the information about vacation tours, luxury travel and exploring the world all in one place. Travel goals was created to give ideas and suggestions thereby making it easier for people to explore destinations, plan vacations and discover places around the world. Our team including tourists and travel agents ready to give you the best support to make your travel experience better. We are always working towards expanding knowledge and scope to provide you with "first class" information to make sure you get the best experience out of your travel.
